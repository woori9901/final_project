WOORI KIM

Scenic Designer/ Production Designer

woorikdesign@gmail.com

Woori Kim (Scenic Designer) is an artist who works for various creative fields in visual arts based on Los Angeles and South Korea. She is currently a BFA3 Experience Design and Production student in the CalArts school of Theater. She is interested in multiple artistic fields, such as music, theater, painting, dance and film, and collaborating with artists who are in other fields. She has previously worked in CalArts Halloween, Center Stage(Scenic Designer, 2021); Independent Cultural Dance Project, Ga Si Ri(Creative Director/Scenic Designer, 2021-22); School of Theater Production, The Great Gatsby(Scenic Designer, 2022).
